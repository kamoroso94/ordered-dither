Ordered Dithering

Experiment with different levels of dithering

Choose an image to upload, select a dithering level, and then hit submit. If the image is large, it may take a few seconds to process the entire image. You can choose between grayscale and color image dithering. Once the image is processed, you can right click the image to "Save as". If you made a mistake, you can reset the form, but you don't have to reset if you just want to change some parameters. I hope you find this tool useful. Ordered dithered images have a special quality that you don't see much nowadays.
